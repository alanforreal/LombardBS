

## Lombard Barbershop for Men Website

### Overview
A classic, traditional-styled barbershop website with online appointment booking functionality. The design will feature warm colors, vintage typography, and a welcoming masculine aesthetic that reflects the authentic barbershop experience.

---

### Pages & Features

#### 1. Homepage
- Hero section with a welcoming headline and call-to-action to book an appointment
- Brief introduction highlighting the shop's identity as an Asian-owned local business
- Featured services preview with icons
- Customer rating display (4.5 stars from 455 reviews)
- Quick-access "Book Now" button

#### 2. Services Page
- **Haircut** - Standard men's haircut with description and pricing placeholder
- **Beard Trim** - Beard trimming and shaping service
- **Hot Towel Shave** - Classic straight razor shave experience
- **Full Service Package** - Complete haircut and shave combo
- Each service card with placeholder images and "Book This Service" buttons

#### 3. Online Booking System
- Date and time selector calendar
- Service selection dropdown
- Customer information form (name, phone, email)
- Confirmation page after booking
- Backend database to store appointments (using Lovable Cloud/Supabase)

#### 4. About Page
- Shop story and history
- "Asian-Owned" business highlight
- Team section with placeholder barber profiles
- Shop atmosphere description

#### 5. Contact Page
- Business hours display (currently shows closes at 9 PM)
- Address: 1212 S Garfield St, Lombard, IL 60148
- Phone: (630) 847-9999
- Embedded Google Maps location
- Contact form for inquiries

---

### Design Style
- **Colors**: Warm browns, cream, gold accents, dark wood tones
- **Typography**: Classic serif fonts for headings, clean sans-serif for body text
- **Elements**: Vintage barbershop motifs (stripes, scissors icons, straight razor imagery)
- **Overall feel**: Welcoming, professional, traditional men's barbershop atmosphere

---

### Technical Requirements
- **Frontend**: React with responsive design (mobile-friendly)
- **Backend**: Lovable Cloud (Supabase) for appointment storage
- **Database**: Appointments table with date, time, service, and customer info
